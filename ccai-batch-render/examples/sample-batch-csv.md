## What I did with the output

1. Renamed videos to match prospect names: `Sarah-Chen-NorthstarCoaching.mp4` etc.
2. Uploaded each to Loom (or bulk upload to my own CDN)
3. Generated unique Loom links per video
4. Added the Loom link to each cold outreach email (from `ccai-cold-outreach`)
5. Sent the batch

Reply rate on the 50 emails: 18% (9 out of 50). The personalized video was specifically mentioned in 7 of the 9 replies.

## Time math

- Skill scaffold + CSV prep: 5 min
- Render 50 videos: 7.2 min
- Bulk upload to Loom: 12 min
- Linking + sending outreach: 25 min
- **Total: 49 min**

Manual production for 50 videos would have been 5-8 hours (15 min/video for filming + editing).

## When this approach breaks

- Beyond ~100 videos: sequential render takes too long. Use `ccai-batch-render-pro` for Remotion Lambda (cloud parallel rendering).
- Heavy compositions (3D, lots of effects): render time per video balloons. Consider simpler templates for batch use.
- One-off creative: not the right use case. Use a video editor.
