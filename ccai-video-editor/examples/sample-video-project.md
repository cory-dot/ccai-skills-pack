## What I did with the output

1. Bulk-uploaded the 50 MP4s to Meta Ads Manager as paused ads
2. Distributed across 10 ad sets (5 ads per set)
3. Each ad set targeted a different interest segment
4. Un-paused all 50 simultaneously for clean A/B
5. After 7 days: kept the top 5 performers, killed the bottom 35, kept 10 in "let it run another week"

Total cost: $0 in tools (Remotion is free, music was already licensed). 50 unique ads in under 30 min of work.

## What this replaces

In a traditional workflow, producing 50 ad variants would require:
- A designer ~$50-150/variant (Fiverr) → $2,500-7,500 total
- OR an in-house creative team ~3-5 days of work
- Plus 30-60 min/variant of upload + targeting setup

With ccai-video-editor + ccai-meta-ads-autopilot:
- ~$0 in production
- ~30 min total (run the skill, edit the data file, render, upload)

## Where I wouldn't use this

- A single hero brand video → use Premiere / hire an editor
- Anything that needs frame-by-frame motion graphics design → use After Effects
- A documentary or interview-style edit → not the right tool

This skill earns its place specifically when you need *many* variations of the *same* template structure.
