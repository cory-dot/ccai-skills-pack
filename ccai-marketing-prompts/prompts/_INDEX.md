## Quick categorization

- **I need to write something today** → category 1 or 2
- **I need to run paid ads** → category 3
- **I want to understand my audience better** → category 4
- **I'm trying to figure out where I fit in the market** → category 5 or 6
- **I have something existing that needs work** → category 7
